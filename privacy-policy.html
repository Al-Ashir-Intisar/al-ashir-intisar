Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 4, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to ShouldIGo. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app to view and rate cafeteria menus.

Information We Collect

  • Google Authentication Data: When you log in with your Google account, we collect basic authentication data such as your email address to verify your identity.
  • Meal Ratings: The ratings you submit are saved to a Google Sheets for calculating an average rating of items in each meal.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your access to the app and a member of our St. Olaf Community.
  • Append your meal ratings to combine with existing ratings dataset.

How We Protect Your Information

We take your data security seriously. All data is handled securely using OAuth2 authentication. We do not store or share your information with any third parties.

Your Control Over Your Data

You have complete control over your data. You can:

  • Revoke app access via your Google account settings.

Third-Party Services

We use Google APIs, including Google Sheets and Google Identity Services, to provide app functionality. These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
